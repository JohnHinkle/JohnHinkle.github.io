About Me

I am a first-year PhD Student at the University of Texas Marine Science Institute in Port Aransas, TX. I graduated from the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill with a Bachelor of Science in Biology and a Certificate in Applied Data Science in May 2024. I am interested in studying deep-sea microbial communities on Earth, and potentially elsewhere.

Research

UTMSI

At UTMSI, my research focuses on the abyssal sediment-hosted microbial communities and how they may be influenced by diffuse hydrothermal fluid flow.

UNC

At UNC, I was a member of the Teske Lab in the Department of Earth, Marine and Environmental Sciences where I studied the microbiology of hydrothermal vents and the deep subsurface in the Guaymas Basin.
